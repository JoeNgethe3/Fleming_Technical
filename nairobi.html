Nairobi

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya. It is the second most populous city in Africa, with a metropolitan area of over 10 million inhabitants.

Standing on the Nairobi River, Nairobi has been a major settlement for five decades, its history going back to its founding by the Maasai, who named it a place of cool waters.
